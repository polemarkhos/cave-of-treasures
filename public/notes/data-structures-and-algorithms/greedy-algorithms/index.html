Greedy Algorithms #

A greedy algorithms constructs a solution to the problem by making the best choice at the time. That’s the greedy part. Instead of looking for a “globally optimal” solution, a greedy algorithm picks the best possible choice each step, which can very often approximate the optimal solution. It does not take back its choices but goes directly to construct the final solution.

The difficulty in designing these algorithms is to find a greedy strategy to produce the best solution.
